This node can interact with a fictive gripper.

The "gripper" grips, by toggling tool_out[0] high, and releases by setting tool_out[1] high.

tool_in[0] reports, that the gripper is currently holding an item, and tool_in[1] reports an error

Gripper live position is expressed by tool analog in 0.
